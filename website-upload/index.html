Welcome to IggyPop Kennel

Elegant Italian Greyhounds raised with love, beauty, and care. We are located in Lexington, Kentucky. We raise show dogs at home with the best and healthiest bloodlines. All dogs will be genetically tested prior to being bred. We raise our puppies and dogs with the highest standards.

Imported Lines

Carefully selected bloodlines with beauty, health, and temperament in mind.

Health Focused

We value responsible breeding, health testing, and proper puppy care.

Family Raised

Our puppies are loved, socialized, and raised like family.
