About Us

IggyPop Kennel was created from a love for Italian Greyhounds and their graceful, affectionate, and playful nature.

We raise Italian Greyhounds with care, socialization, and attention to beauty and temperament. Every puppy is part of the family from day one, and we are proud to share their stories with people who value gentle companionship and elegance.
